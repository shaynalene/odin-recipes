Brownies Recipe

Description

Brownies are my all time favorite dessert food that I would gladly eat at a normal day. I prefer a crispy outer shell with a fudgy inner shell. It's perfect to pair with a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • Chocolates
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Eggs

Steps/Instructions

Note: These steps are based from the popular recipe uploaded on the internet.

  1. Preheat the oven to 325Â°F. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and use a spatula to smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached.
  7. Cool completely before slicing.
